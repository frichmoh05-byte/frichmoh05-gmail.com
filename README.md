SBC-Designed Teaching Materials — VET, FODE & Secondary Schools

Purpose
- Provide Skills-Based Curriculum (SBC) aligned teaching materials adaptable for Vocational Education and Training (VET), Flexible Open and Distance Education (FODE), and Secondary School contexts.

Structure
- `VET/` (sample module) — competency-focused module with practical activities and assessment.
- `FODE/` (sample module) — distance-learning adaptations, self-study tasks, and low-bandwidth options.
- `Secondary/` (sample module) — classroom-focused lessons aligned to SSC learning outcomes.
- `teacher_guides/` — templates and guidance for delivery, differentiation, and assessment.
- `lesson_plans/` — lesson plan templates for SBC lessons with timings, resources, and assessments.
- `assessments/` — formative and summative assessment examples and rubrics.
- `resources/` — student handouts and printable materials.

How to use
1. Adapt the sample modules to local curriculum codes and context.
2. Use the teacher guide template to align learning outcomes, resources, and assessment criteria.
3. For FODE, prefer low-bandwidth resources (printed handouts, SMS/USSD prompts, offline apps).
4. For Secondary, prefer low-bandwidth resources (printed handouts, SMS/USSD prompts, offline apps).
