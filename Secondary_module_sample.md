Secondary Sample Module — Entrepreneurship & Life Skills

Module code: SEC-ENT-301
Level: Lower Secondary (ages 13-15)
Estimated hours: 30

Learning Outcomes
- LO1: Identify simple business opportunities in local contexts.
- LO2: Develop a basic business plan (idea, costing, simple marketing).
- LO3: Demonstrate teamwork, communication, and financial literacy basics.

Lesson Sequence (6 lessons)
- Lesson 1: What is entrepreneurship? (45 mins) — brainstorm local needs.
- Lesson 2: Idea generation and validation (60 mins) — surveys, role play.
- Lesson 3: Simple costing and pricing (60 mins) — group activity.
- Lesson 4: Marketing on a small budget (45 mins) — posters, social media basics.
- Lesson 5: Teamwork and customer service (45 mins) — simulations.
- Lesson 6: Presentation of mini-business plans (90 mins) — peer feedback.

Assessment
- Formative: Observation checklists, peer feedback sheets.
- Summative: Group mini-business plan presentation and simple profit calculation.

Differentiation
- Provide simplified templates for learners needing support; extension tasks for advanced learners.

Resources
- Local market visit, poster materials, calculators, simple template handouts.
