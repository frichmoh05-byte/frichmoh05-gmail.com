FODE Sample Module — Digital Literacy for Lifelong Learning

Module code: FODE-DL-201
Level: Secondary / Adult basic
Estimated hours: 40 (self-study + tutor-facilitated activities)

Competency Outcomes
- CO1: Use basic ICT tools (mobile phone, basic laptop) to communicate and access learning.
- CO2: Create and manage simple digital documents and store them safely.
- CO3: Apply critical evaluation of online information for learning purposes.

Distance-learning Design
- Modular self-study units with clear objectives, summaries, and low-bandwidth media.
- Printed learner guide for learners with intermittent internet access.
- SMS/IVR prompts for weekly reminders and short quizzes.

Sample Unit Structure
- Unit 1: Introduction to devices — 6 hours (print + short video)
- Unit 2: Document creation and management — 12 hours (printed tasks + email submission)
- Unit 3: Online information literacy — 12 hours (case studies, reflective questions)
- Unit 4: Assessment and reflection — 10 hours (portfolio submission)

Assessment & Support
- Formative: Phone/SMS quizzes, tutor feedback on submitted tasks.
- Summative: Portfolio of 3 artifacts (document, email communication, short reflective write-up).
- Support: Local study groups and periodic face-to-face tutorials where possible.

Low-bandwidth tips
- Use compressed images and transcribed captions for videos.
- Distribute content via memory sticks and local study centres.

Teacher/Tutor notes
- Provide clear marking rubrics and scaffolded checklists for learners to self-assess before submission.
