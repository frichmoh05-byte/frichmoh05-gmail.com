Assessments & Rubrics — Examples

1) Practical Performance Rubric (VET — Wiring Installation)
- Criteria: Safety procedures, correct connections, neatness, functional testing.
- Levels: Not yet competent / Developing / Competent / Excellent
- Pass threshold: Competent in all critical safety items + 60% overall.

2) FODE Portfolio Rubric (Digital Literacy)
- Criteria: Completeness, accuracy, application, reflection.
- Levels: Emerging / Developing / Proficient / Distinguished

3) Secondary Group Project Rubric (Entrepreneurship)
- Criteria: Idea viability, costing accuracy, teamwork, presentation.
- Suggested weights: Idea (30%), Costing (25%), Teamwork (20%), Presentation (25%).
