Title: My Mini Business Idea

1. What is the idea? (one sentence)
2. Who are your customers? (describe)
3. What will you sell? (list)
4. How much will it cost to make/buy? (simple table)
5. How much will you sell it for? (simple calculation)
6. What materials or help do you need?
7. What is one thing you will do this week to move the idea forward?

Teacher: collect answers and provide feedback using the project rubric in `assessments/assessment_rubrics.md`.
